Pre-roll automation FAQ

Real answers on cost, output, and compliance.

The questions producers actually ask before automating a preroll floor, answered with the same specs and numbers used across this site, nothing invented for this page.

What is pre-roll automation?+

Pre-roll automation replaces manual rolling, filling, and finishing with a machine that handles those steps at production scale. The Hefestus cannabis line automates rolling, filling, sealing, labeling, and compliance checks, so a single operator can run output that would otherwise take a much larger manual crew.

How many prerolls can an automated machine produce per hour?+

It depends on the machine. AuraX runs up to 2,000 prerolls an hour with a single operator. LumineX, the compact line, runs up to roughly 600 an hour, about 80,000 a month, in a four by four foot footprint built for smaller floors and lower volumes.

How much labor does pre-roll automation actually save?+

AuraX is built to run with a single operator and can cut labor by up to 90% compared to manual rolling, without reducing output.

Can automated pre-roll machines handle infused or sticky material?+

Yes. Both AuraX and LumineX are built to handle infused and sticky material that jams conventional lines, using the patented Dutch Crown fold.

How much does a pre-roll automation machine cost?+

Pricing depends on your configuration, volume, and which machine fits your line. Financing options are available. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What compliance features come with an automated pre-roll line?+

The AuraX Weight Checker automatically rejects underfilled or overweight units before they ship, so every unit that leaves your line is in spec, a common regulatory requirement for cannabis producers.

Should I evaluate a pre-roll machine with a demo?+

Yes, but make sure it runs your own product. Some vendors demo with their own sample material. Switching an active line carries real operational risk, so we run your own material through the machine at production rate and let you see the output, fill quality, and reject rate for yourself before you commit any capital. See how the demo works.

Is there a real example of a producer scaling with pre-roll automation?+

Yes. Seed & Smith in Denver, Colorado scaled daily output from 1,200 to 1,500 prerolls to well past 10,000, cut their rolling crew to two people, consolidated their cone SKUs from three or more down to one, and expanded into infused SKUs, all with zero downtime since installation.

How do I prepare cannabis material for an automated pre-roll line?+

Material prep matters as much as the rolling machine itself. The TerpPreserve Grinder uses a low-heat sift so the material feeding your line keeps its terpene profile, in two throughput models, 75 kg per hour and 150 kg per hour, for different line sizes.
